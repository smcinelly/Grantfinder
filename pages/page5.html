Mid-Atlantic Arts Foundation

Creative Fellows receive $3,000 or $1,500 (depending on the length of stay) in support of their residency. The grant is paid to the respective colony directly on behalf of the artist. In addition, Fellows receive up to $250 in reimbursement for the cost of travel to and from their residency. Deadline: Nov. 2019
