Wag! Offers NYC Animal-Welfare Grants

Helping rescue pets has been part of Wag!’s mission since our founding. Through a partnership with the Rescue Bank, Wag! has donated proceeds from every walk to provide more than 5 million meals and counting to rescue dogs nationwide. Through this new grant program, Wag! is striving to make an impact in communities, like New York City, by supporting the work of local animal-welfare organizations to improve the quality of life for dogs.
Eligibility Requirements for Applicants include; Municipal or county animal shelters and animal control, 501(c)(3) non-profit animal welfare organizations and organizations must be based in upstate New York or Long Island (New York City is excluded from the APCP program).
Wag! will accept applications for the 2018 grant cycle on a rolling basis with the first round of applications being considered starting July 23, 2018. Notification of awards will be announced on an ongoing basis. We highly encourage organizations to apply as soon as possible to increase the opportunity of being awarded a grant. The grant application and guidelines are available here.
