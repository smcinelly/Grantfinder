Second Chance Grants

Financial assistance to help offset the costs of rescuing animals who are homeless or the victims of human cruelty. Every year, countless animals suffer deplorable abuse and neglect. Often, local shelters and rescue groups are saddled with the steep expenses of saving, sheltering, and caring for these animals in desperate need.
